How You Can Help

Every action counts. Discover practical ways to make a positive impact on our environment and join a community of changemakers.

Everyday Actions

Small changes in your daily routine can make a significant difference for our planet.

At Home

  • Reduce energy consumption by using LED bulbs and energy-efficient appliances
  • Conserve water by fixing leaks and taking shorter showers
  • Compost food scraps to reduce methane emissions from landfills
  • Use eco-friendly cleaning products to reduce water pollution
  • Install a programmable thermostat to optimize heating and cooling

While Shopping

  • Bring reusable bags to reduce plastic waste
  • Choose products with minimal packaging or recyclable packaging
  • Support companies with strong environmental commitments
  • Buy local and seasonal foods to reduce transportation emissions
  • Consider second-hand items before buying new

Transportation

  • Walk, bike, or use public transportation when possible
  • Carpool to reduce the number of vehicles on the road
  • Maintain your vehicle properly for optimal fuel efficiency
  • Consider an electric or hybrid vehicle for your next purchase
  • Combine errands to reduce unnecessary trips
